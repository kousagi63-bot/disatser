About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, DisasterGuard began as a small volunteer initiative in response to the increasing frequency of natural disasters. What started with a handful of dedicated responders has grown into a comprehensive disaster management organization operating across multiple regions worldwide.

Today, we coordinate with over 450 partner organizations, deploy rapid response teams within minutes, and provide life-saving resources to communities in crisis. Our mission is simple: no community should face a disaster alone.
